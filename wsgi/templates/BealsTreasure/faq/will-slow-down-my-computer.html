Will this slow down my computer and/or network?

This should not slow down your computer and/or internet. There is a small initial download, used to load the data files. Once this initial download of data is done, there are periodic download updates but these shouldn't be enough to cause your internet to slow down. While this project performs calculations on your machine, the website has been designed to minimize any slow downs of the browser and computer.

If you have issues with the website, please feel free to contact the Captain.
